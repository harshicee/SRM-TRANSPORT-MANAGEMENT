Welcome to the SRM Transport Management System

Our transport management system is designed to make it easy for you to book transportation to and from SRM University. With our system, you can view available routes and schedules, book your transportation in advance, and make payments online.

Route 1

Route 1 is a popular route that connects the airport to the university. It runs every hour from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM.

Download Route PDF

Route 2

Route 2 is a scenic route that connects the university to the beach. It runs every two hours from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM.

Download Route PDF

Route 3

Route 3 is a convenient route that connects the university to the city center. It runs every 30 minutes from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM.

Download Route PDF

Bookings

Booking transportation is easy with our system. Simply select your desired route and schedule, enter your passenger information, and make a payment. You will receive a confirmation email with your booking details.

Payment

Payments can be made online using a variety of payment methods, including credit card, debit card, and net banking. We use secure payment gateways to ensure that your payment information is protected.
